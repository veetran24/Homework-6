Seattle Starbucks Stores Map by Ownership Type


This interactive map dynamically visualizes Starbucks locations present across Seattle for distinguishing company-owned and licensed stores through strategically designed icons. Vibrant green coffee mug markers represent company stores, while darker green shopping carts denote licensed (franchise) locations. This considered iconography rapidly conveys ownership types nuances with visual flair. The Company-Owned Stores represent the direct corporate presence of Starbucks, ensuring consistency and innovation. On the other hand, Licensed Stores are independently owned and operated by third-party businesses. These businesses enter into a licensing agreement with Starbucks, allowing them to use the Starbucks brand, products, and processes. The map is centered on Seattle and utilizes the cartodbpositron tile set for a clean and modern aesthetic. Each marker provides essential information such as store name, address, and ownership type through tooltips, enhancing user engagement. Marker clusters are employed to prevent overcrowding in areas with many stores, ensuring a clear and informative presentation. The Tag Filter feature enables users to toggle between company-owned and licensed stores to help users explore the data. This thoughtful design aims to deliver an intuitive, visually compelling, and informative user experience that brings the data to life. By combining style and substance, the map provides engaging insights into Starbucks' presence across the Emerald City - Seattle.
